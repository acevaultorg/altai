Best Grammarly Alternatives in 2026 (Free & Paid)

Free and paid Grammarly alternatives ranked for grammar checking, writing style, plagiarism detection, and AI writing assistance in 2026.

0 tools reviewed Updated 2026-04-18

Grammarly is the most recognizable name in AI writing assistance, but it's not always the best — or cheapest — option. Competitors like QuillBot have caught up on paraphrasing, Notion AI integrates directly into your workspace, and Claude often produces cleaner prose rewrites for free.

We tested 8 Grammarly alternatives across three dimensions: grammar and clarity fixes, style improvement, and AI-assisted rewriting. Here's what we found.

Bottom line: For grammar checking alone, Grammarly's free tier is still hard to beat. For paraphrasing and rewriting, QuillBot matches or exceeds Grammarly Premium at a lower price. For full-document writing assistance, Claude (free) handles long-form better than either. Premium users who use all Grammarly features should audit whether QuillBot + Claude doesn't cover the same use cases for less.
