Best AI Video Generators in 2026 — Ranked & Tested

8 AI text-to-video tools ranked on quality, length, and price. Runway vs Sora vs Kling vs Pika — which is actually worth paying for?

8 tools reviewed Updated 2026-04-18

AI video generation crossed a quality threshold in 2026. Tools like Runway Gen-3, Sora, and Kling can now produce 5-10 second clips that hold up under scrutiny — consistent faces, coherent motion, cinematic lighting. The race is now about length, cost, and control.
