Best DeepSeek Alternatives in 2026 (Tested & Ranked)

DeepSeek shook the AI world in 2025 — but it's not right for everyone. Here are the 8 best alternatives ranked by quality, privacy, and price.

8 tools reviewed Updated 2026-04-18
