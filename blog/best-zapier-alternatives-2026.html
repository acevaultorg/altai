Best Zapier Alternatives in 2026 — Cheaper & More Powerful

Zapier's pricing jumped. Here are 6 alternatives that do the same job for less — including a free open-source option developers love.

6 tools reviewed Updated 2026-04-18

Zapier was the default no-code automation tool for years — but its 2023 pricing changes pushed many teams to look elsewhere. At $20/mo for 750 tasks, it gets expensive fast.

We looked at 6 alternatives across price, integration count, and AI capabilities. Two stand out above the rest.

1

Make

Visual flow builder with complex branching, iteration, and data transformation. 1,000+ apps. Significantly cheaper than Zapier at scale.

Free / $9/mo Visit Make →
2

n8n

Open-source and self-hostable with no per-execution pricing. AI nodes built in. Best for developers.

Free (self-host) / $20/mo Visit n8n →
3

Replicate

If your automation is AI-heavy (run models, transform content), Replicate's API is purpose-built.

Pay-per-use Visit Replicate →
4

HuggingFace

Spaces + Inference API for AI-specific pipelines. Free tier generous.

Free / $9/mo Visit HuggingFace →
5

Framer

For design/publish automation specifically, Framer's built-in CMS and publish pipeline beats Zapier for marketing teams.

Free / $15/mo Visit Framer →
6

Notion AI

If your Zapier workflows are primarily Notion-based, Notion AI's built-in automations eliminate the middleware entirely.

Free / $10/mo Visit Notion AI →

Bottom line: Make is the best Zapier alternative for most teams — same visual builder, more powerful branching, fraction of the price. n8n wins for developers who want full control and zero per-execution costs. Both are meaningfully cheaper than Zapier at scale.
