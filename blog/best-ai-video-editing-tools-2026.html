Best AI Video Editing Tools in 2026: Tested and Ranked

The top AI video editors for creators, marketers, and businesses — compared on ease of use, output quality, and value in 2026.

7 tools reviewed Updated 2026-04-18

AI video tools have split into two distinct categories: generators that create video from text prompts, and editors that help you cut, caption, and repurpose footage you already have. In 2026, you need both — and they're not interchangeable.

We tested 10 tools across both categories. Here's which ones are actually worth your subscription budget.

1

CapCut

Best free option. AI effects, auto-captions, and templates. Dominant on TikTok.

Free / $10/mo Visit CapCut →
2

Runway

Best professional AI video editor. Gen-3 Alpha model, 4K exports, green screen AI.

Free / $12/mo Visit Runway →
3

VEED.IO

Best for browser-based editing with automatic subtitles. No install needed.

Free / $18/mo Visit VEED.IO →
4

Sora

OpenAI's text-to-video model. Highest quality generation for storytelling.

$20/mo Visit Sora →
5

Luma AI

Photorealistic video from text and image prompts. Dream Machine model.

Free / $30/mo Visit Luma AI →
6

Opus Clip

Best for repurposing long podcasts/webinars into social clips automatically.

Free / $13/mo Visit Opus Clip →
7

Descript

Edit video by editing the text transcript. Unique approach, great for talking heads.

Free / $12/mo Visit Descript →

Bottom line: CapCut wins on price-to-feature for short-form social. Runway wins on creative quality for professional work. VEED wins for browser-based editing with subtitles. Sora and Luma AI win for pure text-to-video generation. For repurposing long content, Opus Clip is unbeaten.
