Poe Reference

Poe by Quora is an AI aggregator letting you chat with Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Llama, Mistral, and 100+ bots in one app. A $20/mo Poe subscription gives access to models that would otherwise cost $60-80/mo combined. Also lets you build and share custom AI bots.

Strengths

  • access 100+ AI models in one place
  • flat monthly price
  • build and share custom bots
  • iOS/Android/Web apps

Weaknesses

  • point limits still apply per model
  • one extra subscription layer
  • features often lag behind native apps
