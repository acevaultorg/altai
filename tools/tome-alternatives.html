Tome Reference

Tome generates polished presentations and narrative documents from a prompt. Combines text and AI-generated images in a scroll-based format. Popular for pitch decks, reports, and proposals.

Strengths

  • beautiful narrative format
  • AI image generation built in
  • fast from prompt to deck
  • web-native sharing

Weaknesses

  • less control than PowerPoint/Slides
  • not ideal for data-heavy slides
  • limited export options
