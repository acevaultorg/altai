Zoom AI Companion Reference

Zoom AI Companion is included at no extra cost with paid Zoom plans. It automatically generates meeting summaries, action items, and can answer questions about what was discussed during or after a call. The catch-up feature lets latecomers get up to speed without interrupting the meeting. It integrates with Zoom Chat, Whiteboard, and Docs.

Strengths

  • Included at no extra cost for Zoom paid subscribers
  • Catch-up feature lets latecomers ask questions mid-meeting
  • Integrated with Zoom Chat, Docs, and Whiteboard
  • Generates summaries and action items automatically
  • Works in both Zoom Meetings and Zoom Phone

Weaknesses

  • Only useful if your team already uses Zoom
  • Requires paid Zoom plan — not available on free tier
  • Less customizable than standalone meeting AI tools
  • Transcript accuracy varies by accent and audio quality
