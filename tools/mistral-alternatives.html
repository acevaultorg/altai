Mistral Le Chat Reference

Mistral AI is a French AI startup that built open-source models matching GPT-4 at a fraction of the size. Le Chat is their free consumer app — fast, multilingual, and GDPR-compliant. Mistral Large 2 and the Mixtral models are open-weight and freely available for self-hosting.

Strengths

  • GDPR-compliant European company
  • fast response times
  • open-source Mixtral available
  • strong French, German, Spanish support

Weaknesses

  • smaller ecosystem than OpenAI
  • no native image generation
  • less brand recognition outside Europe
