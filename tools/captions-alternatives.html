Captions.ai Reference

Captions.ai is a mobile-first video editing app powered by AI. It auto-generates captions, removes filler words, fixes eye contact, and adds animated subtitles to videos.

Strengths

  • accurate auto-captions
  • eye contact correction
  • removes filler words (um, uh)
  • animated subtitle styles

Weaknesses

  • mobile-only (iOS and Android)
  • limited desktop/web features
  • watermark on free tier
