Opus Clip Reference

Opus Clip uses AI to automatically find the most engaging 30-90 second moments in long-form video and reformat them as vertical clips for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts. It adds captions, identifies hook moments, and scores clips by virality potential.

Strengths

  • saves 10+ hours per video repurposed
  • auto-captions with animated text
  • virality score highlights best clips
  • supports YouTube, Zoom, podcast

Weaknesses

  • $9+/mo for meaningful usage
  • AI sometimes misses context
  • clip selection still needs human review
