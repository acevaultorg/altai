QuillBot Reference

QuillBot is a popular AI writing assistant best known for paraphrasing and grammar checking. Free tier rewrites short paragraphs; Premium unlocks full documents, plagiarism checker, and unlimited modes.

Strengths

  • strong paraphrasing with multiple modes
  • grammar checker built in
  • browser extension
  • plagiarism checker on Premium

Weaknesses

  • limited context window on free tier
  • less capable for long-form generation vs Jasper/Copy.ai
  • output can feel mechanical
