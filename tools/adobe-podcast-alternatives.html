Adobe Podcast Reference

Adobe Podcast Enhance Speech uses AI to strip background noise, normalize levels, and make any voice recording sound like it was captured in a professional studio. Upload a clip, download broadcast-quality audio. Part of Adobe Creative Cloud, it also includes AI-powered transcription and a web-based recorder.

Strengths

  • One-click background noise removal — remarkable quality
  • Free tier available (limited minutes)
  • Web-based — no software install needed
  • Integrates with Adobe Premiere and Audition
  • Handles room echo, HVAC noise, keyboard clicks

Weaknesses

  • Free tier limited to a few hours/month
  • Paid tier requires full Creative Cloud subscription
  • No multi-track recording or mixing
  • Transcription less accurate than dedicated tools
