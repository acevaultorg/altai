Ideogram Reference

Ideogram excels at rendering readable text inside generated images — posters, logos, signs, social graphics. Ideogram 2.0 also competes with Midjourney on photorealism. Free tier gives 10 images/day.

Strengths

  • best text-in-image rendering
  • strong photorealism in v2
  • generous free tier
  • fast generation

Weaknesses

  • weaker at complex artistic styles vs Midjourney
  • less community/inspiration feed
  • limited style controls
