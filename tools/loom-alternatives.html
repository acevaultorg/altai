Loom Reference

Loom lets you record your screen and camera, then uses AI to generate a title, summary, chapters, and transcript automatically. The async video tool of choice for remote teams. Acquired by Atlassian in 2023.

Strengths

  • instant AI summary and chapters
  • shareable link in seconds
  • strong free tier
  • Slack and Notion integrations

Weaknesses

  • 5-minute limit on free tier
  • video editing is basic
  • less suited for large video libraries
