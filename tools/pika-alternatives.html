Pika Reference

Pika generates short videos from text or images with strong character animation capabilities. Features like Pikaffects (lip sync, motion effects) set it apart. Popular with content creators and social media marketers.

Strengths

  • character animation
  • lip sync
  • pikaffects motion effects
  • easy interface

Weaknesses

  • shorter clips than Runway/Sora
  • less photorealistic than Kling
  • limited scene complexity
