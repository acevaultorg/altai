Kagi Reference

Kagi is a paid, ad-free search engine with a built-in AI assistant (Kagi Assistant). Because it is subscription-funded, Kagi shows zero ads and does not sell your data. Its AI features include Summarizer (instant summaries of any webpage), FastGPT (quick answers from the web), and the full Kagi Assistant powered by GPT-4, Claude, or Gemini with citations.

Strengths

  • Zero ads — search results ranked purely on relevance
  • No tracking, no data selling, no personalized ad profiles
  • Built-in AI assistant with citations (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini)
  • Webpage Summarizer: summarize any URL instantly
  • User-controlled result blocking and boosting

Weaknesses

  • Paid only — no free tier beyond 100 searches
  • Smaller index than Google — occasional gaps in freshness
  • AI Assistant adds cost to already-paid subscription
  • Less useful for local/map searches than Google
