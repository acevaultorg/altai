NotebookLM Reference

NotebookLM lets you upload documents, PDFs, videos, and websites, then ask questions answered exclusively from YOUR sources — no hallucinations from general training data, only citations from what you uploaded. The Audio Overview feature converts any document into a podcast-style dialogue.

Strengths

  • answers grounded only in your sources
  • PDF/YouTube/Google Doc ingestion
  • Audio Overview podcast generation
  • free for most use cases

Weaknesses

  • limited to uploaded sources (no real-time web search)
  • 50 notebook limit on free plan
  • weaker at coding or creative tasks
