Udio Reference

Udio generates complete songs from text prompts — vocals, instruments, lyrics, mastering. Founded by ex-Google DeepMind researchers, Udio is Suno's main competitor. The free plan gives 100 tracks/month, and output quality rivals paid music production software.

Strengths

  • 100 free tracks/month
  • professional audio quality
  • strong custom vocals
  • full song with lyrics from one prompt

Weaknesses

  • copyright uncertainty for commercial use
  • limited control over individual instruments
  • smaller community than Suno
