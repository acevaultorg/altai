Speechify Reference

Speechify converts any text into natural-sounding speech: PDFs, articles, docs, books, emails. 30+ AI voices, 4.5× normal reading speed, and a mobile app that reads whatever your camera sees. Popular with students, busy professionals, and people with dyslexia.

Strengths

  • Chrome extension reads any webpage
  • dyslexia-friendly

Weaknesses
